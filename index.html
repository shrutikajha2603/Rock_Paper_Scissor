Rock Paper Scissors Game

RULES
  • Select any one option among Rock, Paper, and Scissor
    1. Rock + Paper = You lose
    2. Rock + Scissor = You win
    3. Rock + Rock = Tie
    4. Paper + Rock = You win
    5. Paper + Paper = Tie
    6. Paper + Scissor = You lose
    7. Scissor + Rock = You lose
    8. Scissor + Paper = You win
    9. Scissor + Scissor = Tie
