Bat Ball Stump Game

RULES
  • Select any one option among Bat, Ball and Stump
    1. Bat+Ball = You win
    2. Bat+Stump = You lose
    3. Bat+Bat = Tie
    4. Ball+Bat = You lose
    5. Ball+Ball = Tie
    6. Ball+Stump = You win
    7. Stump+Bat = You win
    8. Stump+Ball = You lose
    9. Stump+Stump = Tie
